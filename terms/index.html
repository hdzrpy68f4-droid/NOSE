What NOSE is, and what it isn't.

Short, in plain English. Using the site means accepting these.

Adults only

NOSE is intended for people aged 21 or over, in places where cannabis is legal. It is your responsibility to know the law where you are.

Aroma, not effects

NOSE compares the aroma shape of terpene panels. It makes no claim about how a product will make you feel, and nothing here is medical, health, or dosage advice. Do not use it to make medical decisions.

Accuracy and limits

Matches are computed from lab data you or a laboratory provide. Reports vary between labs, panels omit compounds, and a certificate describes a tested sample rather than every gram in the jar. A similarity score is a hypothesis about aroma, not a guarantee, and we provide it without warranty.

Your data and content

Palate entries you create stay in your browser and belong to you. You are responsible for having the right to upload any report you submit. See the privacy page for exactly what leaves your device.

Availability

This is an early product offered free. It may change, break, or go offline without notice. Export your palate if it matters to you — we hold no backup.

Liability

To the fullest extent the law allows, NOSE is provided “as is” and we are not liable for purchasing decisions made using it. Nothing here removes rights you have under consumer law where you live.
