Six-family explorer

A faster way to read a terpene panel.

The six-family system groups related aroma signals into a visual spectrum. It is a practical interpretation layer—not a claim that these six buckets explain every molecule in the jar.

Our position

We won’t predict your high.

The relationship between aroma compounds and subjective effects is not settled enough for NOSE to promise outcomes. We compare the part the report can support: aroma profile.
