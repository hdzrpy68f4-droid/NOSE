Compounds used by this model

NOSE currently groups these commonly reported compounds into the herbal family:

  • Terpinolene
  • Ocimene

Profile role

How it can shape the fingerprint

In the NOSE model, herbal signals read green, fresh and airy. They can resemble pine at first, but tend to feel leafier and less resinous.

Important boundary

What the family does not tell you

Herbal is a broad aroma family. The actual jar may also contain minor odorants that are not represented in a standard terpene panel.
