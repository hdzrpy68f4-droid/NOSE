Common panel signals

Compounds used by this model

NOSE currently groups these commonly reported compounds into the spice family:

  • β-Caryophyllene
  • α-Humulene

Profile role

How it can shape the fingerprint

In the NOSE model, spice adds warmth and structure. It often appears beside earthy notes and can sharpen citrus, pine or floral profiles.

Important boundary

What the family does not tell you

A stronger spice share does not predict intensity of effect. It only describes the relative shape of the measured aroma panel.
