Profile role

How it can shape the fingerprint

In the NOSE model, earthy signals often form the base of a profile. They can make brighter citrus, pine or floral notes feel deeper and more grounded.

Important boundary

What the family does not tell you

Earthy is a descriptive family, not a quality grade. Storage, curing and moisture can also change how grounded notes present to the nose.
