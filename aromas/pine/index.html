Common panel signals

Compounds used by this model

NOSE currently groups these commonly reported compounds into the pine family:

  • α-Pinene
  • β-Pinene
  • Fenchol
  • Camphene

Profile role

How it can shape the fingerprint

In the NOSE model, pine signals feel crisp, resinous and green. They can overlap with herbal notes but usually read more evergreen than leafy.

Important boundary

What the family does not tell you

Pine is an interpretation of reported compounds. A lab panel may not include every volatile compound involved in the actual aroma.
