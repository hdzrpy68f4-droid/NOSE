Profile role

How it can shape the fingerprint

In the NOSE model, a citrus-heavy profile reads as bright and lifted. It can sit on top of earthy, spice, floral or herbal signals rather than appearing alone.

Important boundary

What the family does not tell you

Citrus is about measured aroma character. It does not mean the product will taste exactly like fruit or produce a particular effect.
