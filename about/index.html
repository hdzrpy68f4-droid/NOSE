About NOSE

A smaller promise, kept.

NOSE exists to make cannabis aroma data understandable without dressing speculation up as science.

The problem

People often buy by strain name, THC percentage, or a quick recommendation. Those signals can miss the batch-specific aroma profile sitting in the laboratory report.

The product

NOSE converts available terpene values into a visual fingerprint, saves profiles locally, and compares new reports with the jars a person already enjoyed.

The boundary

NOSE does not claim to diagnose, treat, recommend dosage, or predict a high. It is an aroma comparison and education tool for adults.

The privacy approach

The static prototype stores saved palate entries in local browser storage. Users can export, import, or delete that data at any time.
