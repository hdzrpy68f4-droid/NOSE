Privacy

What we collect, in full.

NOSE has no accounts, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. This page lists everything that leaves your device, because a site that promises honesty about terpenes should be equally exact about data.

Your palate stays on your device

Jars you save are written to your browser's local storage. The terpene values, the names you give jars, and the profiles themselves are never uploaded. Clearing your browser data deletes them permanently — we hold no copy and cannot restore them.

You can export your palate to a JSON file, import one back, or delete everything from the matcher at any time.

Match feedback — only when you tap it

When you press “Matched” or “Didn't match” on a result, we receive: the similarity score, its confidence band, how many jars were in your palate, which built-in sample jars were involved, and a timestamp.

Jars you created yourself are reported as the single word custom. Names you typed never leave your browser. There is no account, cookie, or device identifier attached, so these votes cannot be linked to you or to each other.

We collect this for one reason: NOSE assumes that a shared aroma shape predicts shared enjoyment, and that assumption is unproven. These votes are how we test it.

Error reports — automatic

If the site's JavaScript fails, your browser sends us the error message, the script and line it came from, which page you were on, your browser's user-agent string, and a timestamp. It is capped at three reports per page load.

This contains no palate data and nothing you typed. Without it, a bug that breaks the site on your device is invisible to us.

Camera, files, and lab links

QR scanning runs entirely in your browser. The camera stream is never transmitted or recorded, and the camera stops when you leave the tab. Permission is only requested when you choose to scan.

Report files you upload are checked for type and size locally. In this version they are not sent anywhere. Pasted lab links are validated against a list of known laboratory domains before anything is done with them.

Hosting

The site is served by Netlify, which keeps standard server logs that include IP addresses, for security and abuse prevention. That applies to any website you visit and is outside our control; see Netlify's own privacy documentation for their retention practices.

What we never do

  • No advertising, and no selling or sharing of data.
  • No analytics suites, session recording, or fingerprinting.
  • No email address or account required to use the matcher.
  • No claims about effects — and so no health data collected or inferred.

Honest status of this document

  • This describes the current build. If we later add server-side report parsing or any analytics, this page changes before that ships, not after.
  • NOSE is for adults 21+ and is an educational aroma tool. It is not medical advice.
  • Questions or a deletion request: replace this line with a real contact address before launch.
